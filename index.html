This visualization is based on an analyses of tweet collected from the 24th until the 27th of December 2013. The collection consists of 14 million tweets and was analyzed for the frequency of about 500 different text patterns that relate to food. These text patterns don't tell the whole story but give an impression of what people eat during Christmas.

The analysis is done based on the assumption that if two or more text patterns are found in a tweet, that the tweet is composed in the context of food. And the analysis is only done on text patterns in the English language.
